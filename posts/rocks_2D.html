Generating geologically-correct rock shapes in 2D (WIP)

November 15, 2019 (Hopefully)

Geology background

Geologists have been investigating bedrock shapes for many years now and have even classified rock masses and stratification types.

Put a figure here.

I don't remember any geologically-based rock generator, and looking at picture of video games also convince me that what we generate virtually is not realistic. So I decided to experiment with this in 2D.

Method

Start with a poisson distribution and a set of fractures, compute the graph constrained by those fractures.
